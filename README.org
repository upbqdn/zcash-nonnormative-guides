#+TITLE: Non-Normative Zcash Guide Materials
#+AUTHOR: m@rek.onl
#+OPTIONS: toc:nil num:nil

A four-part, layered monograph on the cryptography of [[https://z.cash/][Zcash]]
Orchard. Each guide assumes the ones before it and builds toward a precise,
self-contained account of the Orchard protocol and the Halo 2 proving system it
relies on.

These materials are *non-normative*: they explain and motivate the
constructions, but the [[https://zips.z.cash/protocol/protocol.pdf][Zcash
protocol specification]] remains the sole authority. Where this text and the
specification disagree, the specification is correct.

* The guides

Read in order; each assumes the previous.

1. *Math Guide* (=math-guide.tex=) — the algebraic and number-theoretic
   groundwork: groups, finite fields, elliptic curves, pairings, and the
   relevant hardness assumptions.
2. *Crypto Guide* (=crypto-guide.tex=) — commitments, hashes, signatures,
   zero-knowledge, and the DLP/CDH/DDH hierarchy, developed only as far as
   Orchard and Halo 2 require.
3. *Halo 2 Guide* (=halo2-guide.tex=) — PLONKish arithmetisation, the
   permutation, lookup, and vanishing arguments, the inner-product polynomial
   commitment, and the Halo accumulation trick.
4. *Orchard Guide* (=orchard-guide.tex=) — notes, commitments, nullifiers, the
   commitment tree, the key hierarchy, the Action statement, and the Orchard
   circuit.

Compiled PDFs are committed alongside the sources.

* Scope

The material includes only what lies on the critical path to understanding
Halo 2 and Orchard; off-path generality is deliberately pruned. Everything that
does lie on that path is explained in full.

* Building

Each guide compiles independently with [[https://tectonic-typesetting.github.io/][Tectonic]]:

#+begin_src sh
tectonic -Z shell-escape math-guide.tex
tectonic -Z shell-escape crypto-guide.tex
tectonic -Z shell-escape halo2-guide.tex
tectonic -Z shell-escape orchard-guide.tex
#+end_src

The =-Z shell-escape= flag is required.
